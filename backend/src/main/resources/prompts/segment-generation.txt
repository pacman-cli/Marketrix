{
  "name": "string - segment name",
  "tagline": "string - one-line description",
  "demographics": {"age_range": "", "income_level": "", "education": "", "location": ""},
  "psychographics": {"values": [], "lifestyle": "", "pain_points": []},
  "behavioral_signals": ["array of observable behaviors"],
  "preferred_channels": ["array of marketing channels"],
  "viability_score": 0.0-1.0,
  "rationale": "string - why this segment is relevant"
}

Guidelines:
- Each segment must be distinct and non-overlapping
- Viability score reflects how reachable and valuable the segment is for this specific startup
- Preferred channels should be actionable (e.g., "Instagram Reels", "LinkedIn thought leadership", "Reddit communities")
- Behavioral signals should be observable and targetable
